Welcome to Hub Thai!

At Hub Thai, we are passionate about bringing you the finest Thai cuisine, crafted with care and tradition. Since our establishment in 2017, we have been delighting taste buds with authentic dishes that celebrate the rich flavors of Thailand.

About Us

Hub Thai restaurant is the newest gem in New York for those craving authentic and delectable Thai cuisine. Conveniently located at 50 Avenue A, we are just a short stroll from Tompkins Square Park.

Step inside, and you'll feel the charm of a French café mixed with the warmth of an English bistro. The atmosphere is enhanced by our friendly and attentive staff, complemented by the inviting aromas wafting from our kitchen—creating a true Thai dining experience.

Our chef treasures the opportunity to introduce local diners and tourists to the vibrant flavors that characterize Thai food. With a careful blend of fresh ingredients, aromatic herbs, and spices, every dish is crafted to showcase the essence of Thai culinary traditions.

Hailing from a family deeply rooted in the catering business in the Chumpon region of Thailand, our chef brings generations of expertise to the table, ensuring that every bite reflects the best of traditional Thai cooking.

At Hub Thai, every guest is greeted with a warm and friendly welcome. Whether you’re here to indulge in a delicious Thai meal, enjoy a quiet drink at the bar, or pick up a meal to go, we are always ready to serve you.
